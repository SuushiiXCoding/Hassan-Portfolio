My Story

I am someone who's always been interested in the creative field since I was in my teens. I found myself to be very observant to things in life and drew a lot which led me down the path of design. I studied Architecture as my Bachelors Degree and learnt a lot from it. However, I decided to change my career path into UX Design and aim to become a UX Designer. It not only solves the problems for websites and apps, it can be implemented into real life situations such as understanding and improving the way a person would experience themselves navigating a store. This brings me to why I believe my previous experience as an architect is a great benefit to my career as a UX Designer. An architect goes through almost the same process; Concept, Development, and Outcome. I am able to critically think, go through the design thinking process and meet the clients needs.
